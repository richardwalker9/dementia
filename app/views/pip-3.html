Personal Independence Payment

Personal Independence Payment (PIP) helps with some of the extra costs caused by long-term ill-health or a disability.

Based on what we know about you, we think you are entitled to £139.75 a week

This is because:

  • you are aged between 16 to 64
  • you have a long-term health condition or disability and difficulties with ‘daily living’ or getting around
  • you are in Great Britain
  • you are habitually resident in the UK, Ireland, Isle of Man or the Channel Islands
  • you are not be subject to immigration control
  • you have had these difficulties for 3 months and expect them to last for at least 9 months

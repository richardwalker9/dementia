Personal Independence Payment

Personal Independence Payment (PIP) helps with some of the extra costs caused by long-term ill-health or a disability.

We can use some of your documents and information about you to help you apply for PIP:

  • Proof of adress
  • Your personal details and contact information
  • Information about your doctor and your medical history
  • Information about your doctor and your medical history

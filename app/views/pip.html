Helping you with dementia

Hello Linda.

You are visiting this page because you have been diagnosed with dementia by your doctor.

You will find services that you might find useful on this page.

You can also talk to a support worker if you need to.

You can use this service on your own, or you can ask a friend or family member to help you.

Services


There are a number of services which people with dementia often find useful. You can find them in the list below.

Not everyone will dementia will need to use all of these services.

  • Lasting Power of Attorney
  • Court of Protection
  • Personal Independence Payment
  • Carer's Allowance

    • Talk to a support worker

    If you need to talk to a support worker, you do this through this page.
